Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Piano Blocks handles information when you use the mobile game. The core gameplay is offline and does not require an account, login, or user profile.

Information We Collect

Piano Blocks does not ask you to create an account and does not directly collect your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, or precise location.

The app may process limited device and gameplay-related information for the following purposes:

  • Local gameplay settings, such as music preference, selected difficulty, and score state.
  • Device identifiers, including Identifier for Vendor, to initialize the integrated security SDK.
  • Advertising-related identifiers and ad interaction information when advertising SDKs are available and permitted by your device settings.
  • Technical information needed by third-party SDKs to provide security, fraud prevention, ad loading, reward validation, diagnostics, and service stability.

Tracking Permission

On supported iOS versions, Piano Blocks may request permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt before accessing tracking-related advertising identifiers. You can allow or deny this request. You can also change your choice later in iOS Settings.

Third-Party SDKs

Piano Blocks integrates third-party SDKs for app security and advertising. These SDKs may collect and process information according to their own privacy policies and technical requirements.

  • HSSecurity SDK for security, fraud prevention, and service stability.
  • Chartboost SDK for advertising and monetization services.
  • Yandex Mobile Ads SDK for rewarded advertising.

How Information Is Used

Information may be used to operate the game, remember local settings, protect the app from abuse, load advertisements, validate rewarded ads, measure ad performance, diagnose technical issues, and comply with applicable platform requirements.

Data Storage

Gameplay settings are stored locally on your device. Third-party SDKs may store or transmit technical information according to their own policies. Piano Blocks does not operate a separate user account database for the game.

Children's Privacy

Piano Blocks is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact the developer through the support contact listed on the App Store product page.

Your Choices

You can disable tracking permission in iOS Settings, limit ad tracking through Apple's privacy controls, turn music on or off inside the game, and delete the app to remove locally stored game settings from your device.

International Processing

Third-party SDK providers may process information in countries or regions outside your own. Their handling of information is governed by their respective privacy policies and applicable laws.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when the app changes. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated when material changes are made.

Contact

For privacy questions, please contact the developer through the support contact listed on the App Store product page.
